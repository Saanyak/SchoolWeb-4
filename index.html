School Library

Built Inside School

The Schafer Park Library is inside the school! You can check out up to three books every week using your card they give you, when you return your books you could check out new ones.

Built Inside School

The Schafer Park Cafatiria is pretty big with about eight to ten large tables in it. They provide delicous lunch meals there that you may like to try.
